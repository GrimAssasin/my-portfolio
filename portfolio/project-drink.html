Case Study

Beverage Product Commercial

Bold pack-shot storytelling crafted for launch ads and social campaigns.

This commercial visualization project was designed to elevate beverage packaging through cinematic contrast, controlled reflections, and energetic product framing. The objective was to deliver campaign-ready visuals that can scale across advertising, launch content, and e-commerce placements.

Mood Board and Creative Direction

Visual direction was based on modern beverage advertising with bold contrast, atmospheric gradients, and confident product-first staging.

Lighting references centered on liquid appeal, specular readability, and strong brand-color separation.

Material decisions targeted realistic packaging behavior while keeping a polished cinematic finish for commercial use.
